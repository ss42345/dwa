P4 Proposal

Stock Trading Signals

Description

This is a continuation of my P3 project with some new additional features.

The main idea is to come up with a simple tool that will tell user whether he/she should buy or sell a particular stock. This analysis will take into account the stock history, the current market conditions and some custom numerical metrics to come up with the final recommendation.

As compared to p3, here are some additional features:

  • Users will have to setup an account and login
  • Users can choose the stock ticker symbol. The stock history data will be downloaded automatically
  • Users can choose some additional metrics like MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence) which will provide additional trading signals.
  • A superimposed chart of the price history and the chosen metrics will be shown in the chart area. This will give users a better visual cue.
  • Time permitting, additional capability will be added that will allow users to create a watch-list of stocks.
